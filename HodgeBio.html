Kathryn Hodge is a Computer Science major and Film minor in her last year at Vassar College. She came to Vassar not really knowing what she wanted to do, but took a computer science class on a whim. Now, she creates YouTube tutorials for people that want to learn how to code and attends as many hackathons as she can. In January, she will begin working at NBCUniversal as a part of Media Labs, which helps integrate new technologies into all of the business units, and in July, she'll begin her journey as a NexTech associate. Overall, she enjoys developing and coding, but she really wants to build things that make an impact.
